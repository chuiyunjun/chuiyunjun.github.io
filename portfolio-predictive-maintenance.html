ML-based analysis platform for building maintenance services in HVAC systems




Intorduction


In order to provide mechanical and HVAC service to our customers, Modern Niagara Group today rely on a a) a scheduled service approach and b) a customer's local building operator to recognize and/or triage issues and place a call to our 24/7 dispatch for reactive service. They are moving toward a more connected service approach wherein an analytics software platform will be in constant communication with customer sites and using rules-based analysis and eventually machine-learning based algorithms will be able to identify anomalies or inefficiencies in customer HVAC and Controls systems, triage urgency and dispatch service (or suggest remote service) before the customer even knows they need it.

We aim to design and build the infrastructure for what will eventually be our AI/ML based analytics platform. This system will interact with mechanical, electrical, and IT systems and touches multiple engineering disciplines outside of the IT space.
